[The episode begins with a white screen, quickly fading out to reveal Fionna's nervous face, then relaxing. She appears to be sitting in a room. Behind her, we see the Adventure Time titlescreen sword, the ribbon sword, and the crystal sword. There's also several green blankets piled up behind her, and a bookshelf with books and various trinkets on it. The camera then expands, revealing the rest of the messy room.]

Fionna: [Stands up, looking around the room.] Huntress?

[She walks over to a small mirror on the bookshelf and looks at herself. With an angry expression, she turns the mirror away from her face. The camera pans over to a miniature version of Marshall Lee's van. Next to it is one of Gary's pastry menschens, a small glass with water and a small plant in it, a hairclip with a daisy on it, a pink rubber chicken, and a mixtape labeled, "M. LEE DEMO." Fionna picks it up and grunts, and then proceeds to shove it in an open drawer. The rubber chicken's neck gets stuck, and Fionna grunts as she's trying to close the drawer. The camera zooms out more, revealing that the room is the shape of Fionna's head. She continues trying to shove the drawer closed.]

Huntress Wizard: [Climbing in through the circle shaped window.] Hi.

[Fionna gasps, and the camera zooms out farther showing the room shaped as Fionna's head right next to a room shaped like Huntress Wizard's head. The camera quickly cuts to the real world, where Huntress sits next to Fionna, her nerve extensions in the sides of Fionna's head. It cuts back to Fionna's mind room, and Huntress fully jumps through the window. She walks past Fionna and approaches a cardboard box, where she picks up and throws several items out of the box.]

Fionna: [Turns toward Huntress.] What are we looking for?

Huntress Wizard: [Stops picking up and throwing items, now holding a snowglobe.] Something that connects you to the green magic of your world. [Throws the snowglobe.] Like, I've got this dark corner in the back of my mind where magic flows. You must have something similar... [She starts feeling around the wall. Fionna approaches her.] Though, it could be symbolized by anything...

[Fionna places her hands on either side of Huntress's head, and they fade into it. Huntress gasps. The camera fades to a dark void where Fionna now stands. The front of her body is lit up by firelight.]

Fionna: Wha...

[It then shows a wooden cabin in flames in front of Fionna.]

Fionna: Is my mind inside Huntress's mind in my mind? [She puts her hand on her head in confusion.]

[A young female's voice is heard crying from inside the cabin, and the flames flare, knocking out the door from the inside. Fionna runs up onto the deck and inside of the burning cabin.]

Huntress Wizard (young form): Open! Open! [She bangs on the door insistently.] I'm never gonna get back home. I won't be able to save Finn. [Orange tears stream down her face.]

Fionna: I'm sorry.

[Huntress Wizard turns to face Fionna, and Fionna sits down beside her.]

Fionna: Everything sucks.

[FIonna picks up and holds the small Huntress as the flames spread closer. Several of Huntress's past memories flash. The flames die out, and the door behind the two starts glowing blue. Fionna, Huntress in her arms, quickly scuttles away from the door. Just after, the door creaks open, blue light pouring out. An otherwordly white deer-like creature with a rose budding out of the top of its head sticks its long neck through the doorframe, and looks over at them.]

Huntress Wizard (young form): The Heart. I thought the way to The Heart would be within you. [Fionna sets her down.] But... it was in my inner mope.

[Huntress walks towards the creature as it leans it's head down.]

Huntress Wizard (young form): [Holds out a small knife.] I'm sorry.

[The creature leans its head up, exposing the underside of its long neck. Huntress pulls her knife up to it, but hesitates. She decides not to, and throws her knife on the floor. She curls up and lays on the floor, and the creature rubs its snout against her head. Huntress transforms back into her original form, and the creature uses it's blue tongue to lick the tears off of her face.
